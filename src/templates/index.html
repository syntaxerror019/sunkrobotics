Innovating Underwater & Surface Robotics for Industry, Research & Exploration

Who We Are

We're Sunk Robotics, a student-run robotics team based in Medford, Massachusetts. Our team is passionate about underwater engineering, hands-on design, and pushing the limits of robotics. Collaboration and innovation drive everything we do.

What We Do

From building Remote Operated Vehicles (ROVs) to developing and testing new robotic solutions, we design and fabricate technology for real-world challenges. Each project meets new criteria and overcomes different challenges.

Our History

Since our founding in November of 2020, Sunk Robotics has developed multiple underwater engineering solutions. Our current lineup includes STEVE ROV, JENA float, JONA ROV, and we are currently developing our latest ROV 2026 (BOB ROV).

Our Journey

2020 – The Beginning

Sunk Robotics was founded by a group of passionate engineering student that were part of the MVTHS Engineering & Robotics shop

in Medford High School.
2022 – First ROV

Built and tested our first Remote Operated Vehicle (STEVE ROV) for competition use in the 2022 MATE ROV Competition to represent our school.

2024 – World Champions

In 2024, Sunk Robotics placed 9th in the International ROV Competition and won the NE MATE ROV Regional.

2025 – Growth

The team has decided to part ways from MATE ROV and focus on our latest ROV 2026 (BOB ROV) designed to complete deep-sea educational and experimental missions.
